# Landing Support Mailto Accessibility Polish

## Summary
Applied small accessibility polish to the landing footer support contact link.

## Change
- Added `aria-label="Email DocuMint AI support"` to the `mailto:support@documint.ai` anchor on the landing page footer.

## Why this matters
- Improves screen-reader clarity for support contact actions.
- Keeps UX semantics strong on trust-critical support paths.
