# Sticky Conversion Bar (High-Intent)

## Summary
Added a high-intent conversion bar near the bottom of the landing page to capture buyers who scroll through pricing and proof sections.

## Implemented
- New conversion-focused strip with clear trial offer framing.
- Two tracked CTAs:
  - `Start Free Trial`
  - `See Plans`
- Reused existing event instrumentation via `TrackedLink`.

## Expected Impact
- **Acquisition:** Better conversion capture from high-intent scrollers.
- **Activation:** More users enter auth/signup flow after reading value proof.
- **Paid conversion:** Clear trial framing improves plan consideration momentum.
- **Retention:** Better-informed signups are more likely to align with product fit.

## Risk
- Copy should be validated with live CTA performance data and adjusted per funnel behavior.
